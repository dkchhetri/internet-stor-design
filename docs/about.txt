7 dimensions of internet-stor.

Guiding principle on which internet-stor is built.

1. Simple to deploy, use and manage.

2. Data Protection and availability through replication.

3. Data Integrity through checksum and self-healing.

4. Checkpointing through versioning.

5. Designed to run on heterogenious and commodity hardware.

6. Extensible using HTTP based Data and Management API.

7. Massively scalable in space and performance through decentralized architecture.
